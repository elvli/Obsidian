Philippians 12:1
For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain

Live in a way to remember it’s not about the things of this world. Because of his resurrection, we can live according to the life that flows from him.

1. Live victoriously 
	1. We have a reason to have joy, to be hopeful, to love our enemies
	2. Jesus redefines that living actually is
2. Live in urgency
	1. If all of our hope is in the rumblings of this world, it’s not going to last
3. Live with right standing
	1. Be steadfast, immovable
