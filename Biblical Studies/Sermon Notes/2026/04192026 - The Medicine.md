Daniel 9 

This is a story of Gods restoration, Daniel’s repentance is very different from other repentance stories.

Why is repentance so important that three leaders, when working God, did it?

What is the role of repentance besides letting go of sin? How does it bring us closer to God?

Repentance brings 3 things:
1. Urgency
	1. Matthew 16:24-28
	2. Daniel 9:6-8
	3. Even though the 70 years was fulfilled, it’s not a time to not care about God and our standing
	4. This was a call for even more urgency to be right with God
	5. We must be right with God, that is more important than relief
2. Sincerity
	1. Daniel’s repentance was with the understanding of urgency which brought him to sincerity
	2. To surrender to him and experience and witness what God can, and is willing, to do in your life. **You need to involve him more in your life**. Repent and turn from you sins. Remember what you being saved from.
	3. We need to involve him to feel him in our life
	4. If you are sunder in your faith, then it will show by your fruits
	5. Sometimes we’re not sincere because our focus is in something else
3. Change
	1. We are not looking back to our sin. Not reminding of the moments we felt good or felt comfortable.
	2. Daniel 9:24-25
	3. Are we ready to change our are we comfortable

We need to ask and remind ourselves the purpose of repentance often
