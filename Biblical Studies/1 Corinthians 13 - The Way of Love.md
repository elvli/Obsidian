### Context

The Corinthian church is super messed up. The church was arguing over and divided a bunch of things like leadership, lawsuits, food sacrifices, spiritual gifts. With the last chapter, we saw how they were using their gifts to compete with each other. Showing off, claiming superiority over one another.

### v.1-3

Paul argues that extraordinary spiritual gifts (speaking in tongues, prophecy, wisdom, knowledge, faith, or sacrificial giving) are meaningless noise and worthless without love. Love is the "most excellent way" and the motivation that validates all actions.

Even self-sacrifice can be self-centered (Matthew 6:2)

### v.4-7

Paul talks about what love is

Paul personifies love.

What Love IS:
- Patient
- Kind
- Rejoices with truth
- Bears all things
- Believes all things
- Hopes all things
- Endures all things

What Love is NOT:
- Envious
- Boastful
- Arrogant
- Rude
- Self-seeking
- Easily angered
- Resentful
